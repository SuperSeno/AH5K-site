About AH5K

AH5K stands for Ahmed Farag and Hady Abou El Kheir, the founders, and 5K represents their five kids. AH5K owns and operates franchise stores, starting with Teaspoon. We currently have three locations and are continuously expanding. Our mission is to expand and bring delightful experiences to our customers through our growing number of stores.
